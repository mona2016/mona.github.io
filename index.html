Hi, I am Mona Mishra

UX Designer at ExxonMobil

I am holistic user experience professional with a mind that is both creative and analytical. I like creating beautiful things and improving what is around me. Sometimes, I also like to mentor people who are new to this profession.

I work with complex systems, designing interfaces via prototyping interaction concepts. I have 3 years experience collaborating on development and UX design projects from concept through completion for clients in IT, education, wellness, healthcare, non-profit and energy sectors. My goal is to develop my existing skills by taking advantage of the growing technology and by learning from the talented professional I get to work with.

I am unable to share my work from ExxonMobil due to its proprietary nature. But you can browse through some of my other interesting projects.

Skills

Research

I employ user research techniques like contextual inquiry, surveys, interviews and card sorting to understand the problem and gather requirements from users and stakeholders. Using usability and heuristic evaluation techniques, I analyze the current design and perform competitive analysis to study the current market for my clients. Lastly, I use affinity diagram and R programming for analyzing large amount of data.

Design

Scenarios, personas and storyboards guide my ideation process, which is followed by wireframing, prototyping and user testing. I am skilled at various design and prototyping tools as well as animation design for mobile apps.

Development

I have a Bachelors in Computer Science and two years of work experience in the software industry. I have worked on several technologies and some of my most recents languages that I have worked on are HTML/CSS, JavaScript, Java, Python and AngularJS (in the order of strongest first).
